How to Stay Safe

🔗

Always check the URL

Real Instagram is always instagram.com. Any other domain is fake.

🔐

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Even if your password is stolen, 2FA stops attackers from logging in.

🚫

Never click suspicious links

If someone sends you a login link via WhatsApp or SMS — it's almost always a scam.

🧠

Use a Password Manager

Password managers auto-fill only on the real website — they won't fill in a fake page.

📢

Report phishing attempts

Forward suspicious links to your IT team or report them to the platform directly.
